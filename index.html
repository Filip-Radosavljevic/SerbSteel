What is Serbsteel?
SerbSteel is a brand that mediates Serbian motives such as history, tradition, religion, knowledge and culture. This website is made for free education by returning to the domestic knowledge and withstanding the others’ motives. Together with the education and the other views of our brand, there is only one purpose and that’s keeping the history, tradition and heroes. The essence of why this website is in English is because this is for all the people in the world because we are here to proudly promote our people’s motives. Especially for the ones who are forgetting where they came from and why it is important. By practicing old values ​​we create a bright future for our descendents.
“The pillar of old values ​​is the foundation of new fashion.” -SerbSteel.
